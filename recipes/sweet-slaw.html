Sweet Slaw

Ingredients

  • 1 (16 ounce) bag coleslaw mix
  • 2 tablespoons diced onion
  • ⅔ cup creamy salad dressing (such as Miracle Whip™)
  • ½ cup white sugar
  • 3 tablespoons vegetable oil
  • 1 tablespoon white vinegar
  • ½ teaspoon poppy seeds
  • ¼ teaspoon salt

Steps

  1. In a separate small bowl, whisk together the mayonnaise, apple cider vinegar, honey, and Dijon mustard until smooth.
  2. In a large mixing bowl, combine the shredded cabbage and grated carrot.
  3. Pour the dressing over the cabbage and carrot mixture and toss until everything is evenly coated.
  4. Season with salt and pepper to taste.
  5. Cover the slaw and refrigerate for at least 30 minutes before serving to allow the flavors to meld together.
