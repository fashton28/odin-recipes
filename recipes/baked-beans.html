Baked Beans

Ingredients

  • Bacon
  • Great Northern Beans
  • Homemade BBQ sauce

Steps

  1. Preheat your oven to 350°F (175°C).
  2. In a mixing bowl, combine the barbecue sauce, brown sugar, molasses, Dijon mustard, chopped onion, and cooked bacon (if using).
  3. Drain and rinse the canned beans, then add them to the mixing bowl and stir until the beans are evenly coated with the sauce mixture.
  4. Transfer the mixture to a baking dish and spread it out evenly.
  5. Cover the baking dish with foil and bake in the preheated oven for 45-60 minutes, or until the beans are hot and bubbly.
  6. Remove the foil during the last 15 minutes of baking to allow the top to caramelize slightly. Season with salt and pepper to taste before serving.
